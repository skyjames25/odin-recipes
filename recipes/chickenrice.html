Chicken Rice

Description

Aromatic Hainanese Chicken Rice

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs
  • White rice/li>
  • Scallions, ginger and garlic
  • Light soy sauce, lime and sesame oil
  • Cucumber.
  1. Marinate the chicken
  2. Fry the aromatics
  3. Fry the rice
  4. Cook the chicken with the rice
